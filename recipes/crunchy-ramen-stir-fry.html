Crunchy Chicken Ramen Stir Fry

Description

A delightful chicken stir fry with ramen noodles. A little sweet, a little spicy, all delicious.

Recipe from Budget Bytes.

Ingredients

Stir Fry Sauce
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
Sriracha Mayo Drizzle
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp sriracha
Stir Fry
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 14oz bag coleslaw mix
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp grated fresh ginger
  • 3 green onions
  • 2 blocks ramen noodles

Steps

  1. Prepare stir fry sauce by mixing ingredients together in a bowl. Set aside.
  2. Prepare mayo drizzle by mixing ingredients together in a bowl. Set aside.
  3. Cube chicken into small bites
  4. Mince 2 cloves of garlic and grate the ginger and slice the green onions
  5. Mash and crush the ramen while still in the bag. Open bags and discard seasoning packet.
  6. Heat skillet to medium-high heat and add cooking oil. Add chicken and saute until cooked through (3-5 minutes). Add garlic and ginger halfway through cooking chicken.
  7. Add coleslaw mix and continue to saute for 2 minutes, until cabbage just begins to soften. Do not overcook.
  8. Add stir fry sauce and mix together, continue to saute for 1 minute.
  9. Turn off heat and stir in crushed ramen.
  10. Serve in a bowl, add green onions and sriracha mayo drizzle to taste
