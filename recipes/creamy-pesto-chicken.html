Creamy Pesto Chicken

Description

A creamy chicken dish with plenty of sauce for noodles

Recipe from Budget Bytes

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil, divided
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pint grape tomatoes, sliced in half
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup pesto
Steps
  1. Fillet the chicken breasts into thinner pieces. Season with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. Heat skillet over medium heat. Add 1 tbsp oil and coat the skillet. Add chicken and cook on both sides until cooked through (about 7-10 minutes total). Set aside.
  3. Turn heat down to medium-low and add remaining oil with garlic and grape tomatoes. Saute for 5 minutes or until tomatoes start to soften.
  4. Add the heavy cream and pesto and stir to combine. Allow sauce to simmer. Taste the sauce and adjust salt/pepper to taste.
  5. Add chicken back to the skillet and simmer for a few minutes, turning to coat the chicken.
